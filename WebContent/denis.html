Angels

Angels is a 1983 novel by American author Denis Johnson. It was Johnson's first novel; previously, he had published several books of poetry.
Alice Hoffman, writing for the New York Times, referred to the novel as "a mixture of poetry and obscenity".

Tree of Smoke

Tree of Smoke is a 2007 novel by American author Denis Johnson which won the National Book Award for Fiction[1][2] and was a finalist for the Pulitzer Prize.[3] It is about a man named Skip Sands who joins the CIA in 1965, and begins working in Vietnam during the American involvement there. The time frame of the novel is from 1963 to 1970, with a coda set in 1983.
